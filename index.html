Welcome to Joe Security, Steelwork & Maintenance



Our Company


Joe Security and Maintenance is a two-man operation. We work on fixing, as well as installing security measures for our customers. We do electric fencing, gate motors, intercoms and many more. We also work in construction and do maintenance for buildings of any size.


Joe Security and Maintenance does woodwork and steel work. We build custom wood cupboards and install them. We also build custom steel gates to the customers specification and measurements.


Our company is built from a vast array of experience. We aim to provide full service and maintenance custom tailored to your request.


We are able to make your life feel more secure with our custom made products which are specifc to your needs. We will are able to install any security measures you require, from electic fences to custom built safes. We are a company capable of any maintanace required as well as construction of any magnitude. We specialize in Steelwork as well as Woodwork.


Welcome to Joe Security, Steelwork & Maintenance is providing a quality service at competitive prices. We are able to offer the personal service which, even in these modern times, is still highly valued by many customers.

Carports - Repairs - Restitching of Shadenets - Replacing of Nets
